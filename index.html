Standard Curriculam

Kinder (3-6) Years

Kindergarten curriculum focuses on helping children learn letters, sounds, words, and basic math skills. It also helps children develop social skills, emotional skills, and creativity.

Elementary School

The elementary school curriculum is designed to build a strong foundation in core academic areas while fostering social, emotional, and physical development. The specific curriculum may vary by country, state, or school district.

Middle (10-16) Years

The Middle Years Curriculum covering Grades 6 to 8 or Years 7 to 9, depending on the country builds on the foundational knowledge from elementary school while introducing more complex concepts, critical thinking, and independent learning.
